Your Oral Health, Our Priority

At Oral Surgery + we are dedicated to providing exceptional oral and maxillofacial surgical care with a personal touch. Our highly skilled team, led by experienced surgeons, is committed to ensuring your comfort and delivering the best possible outcomes for all your oral health needs.

Our practice specializes in a wide range of oral surgical procedures, including wisdom teeth extraction, corrective jaw surgery, and the treatment of oral diseases and facial trauma. What we do.

At Oral Surgery + we specialize in performing wisdom teeth extractions. Our efficient framework and specialized training enable our team of surgeons and nurses to handle a significantly higher volume of wisdom teeth extractions than an independent surgeon typically could in a single day.

How.

Our oral surgeries are meticulously planned and executed to ensure the best outcomes. The process starts with an initial consultation, where your medical history and specific needs are reviewed. Advanced imaging techniques like X-rays or 3D scans help in precise surgical planning.

On the day of surgery, anesthesia is administered for your comfort. The surgeon makes a small incision in the gum tissue to expose the tooth and surrounding bone. The tooth is then carefully extracted, sometimes in sections for easier removal. The area is cleaned, and the incision is closed with stitches.

Postoperative care includes detailed instructions on managing pain, swelling, and proper healing. Follow-up appointments ensure you are recovering well and provide any additional care needed. Our thorough approach ensures a safe and effective oral surgery experience. 

Why.

Oral surgery is essential for addressing complex dental issues that cannot be resolved with general dental care. Here are some key reasons why oral surgery might be necessary:

  • Wisdom Teeth Removal: Impacted or misaligned wisdom teeth can cause pain, infection, and damage to adjacent teeth. Removing them prevents these complications.
  • Corrective Jaw Surgery: Misaligned jaws can impair chewing, speaking, and breathing. Corrective surgery improves function and facial appearance.
  • Treating Oral Diseases: Conditions like cysts, tumors, and severe infections require surgical intervention for thorough treatment and prevention of further health issues.
  • Alleviating Chronic Pain: Issues such as TMJ disorders and severe gum disease may need surgical solutions to relieve pain and improve quality of life.

At Oral Surgery + of Lake Charles and Wisdom Teeth of Louisiana, our specialized expertise and advanced techniques ensure effective, safe, and comfortable oral surgery care.

Our Services

Wisdom Teeth

Wisdom Teeth

Wisdom teeth, also known as third molars, typically emerge in late adolescence or early adulthood. For many people, these teeth can cause various oral health issues and may need to be removed. At Oral Surgery + of Lake Charles, we specialize in the safe and effective extraction of wisdom teeth to prevent complications and maintain your oral health.

Bone Grafting

Bone Grafting

Bone grafting is a vital procedure in restorative and reconstructive oral surgery, designed to enhance the stability and volume of your jawbone. At Oral Surgery + we perform bone grafting to prepare the jaw for dental implants, repair bone loss, and improve overall oral health. 

Learn More
Facial Trauma

Facial Trauma

Facial trauma, whether from accidents, sports injuries, or other incidents, can have significant physical and emotional impacts. At Oral Surgery + of Lake Charles, our skilled team is dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate care for all types of facial injuries, ensuring optimal recovery and restoration of function and appearance.

Pre-Prosthetic Surgery

Pre-prosthetic surgery is essential for preparing the mouth for dentures or other dental prosthetics. At Oral Surgery + of Lake Charles, we provide expert surgical services to ensure a comfortable and stable fit for your new dental appliances, enhancing both function and aesthetics.

Impacted Canines

Impacted Canines

Canine teeth, or cuspids, play a crucial role in your dental structure and overall oral health. When these teeth become impacted, meaning they do not emerge properly through the gum, they can cause a range of problems.
